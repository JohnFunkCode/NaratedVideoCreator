Staying safe also includes physically securing your device.

Lock it with a passcode or biometrics and set a screen timeout to automatically lock when it's not in use.   This will prevent strangers from picking it up and doing something bad with it.   It's common for people to steal phones so they can get to un-protected banking apps.   Criminals also steal phones to plant malware on them, and then return them feigning to be a good guy who returned your lost phone.

Enable Auto-Updates - We already talked about the importance of staying up-to-date.

Also make sure your phone is backed up!   Automatic backups are the most reliable way to ensure you don’t lose everything if your phone breaks or is lost or stolen.

And, Lastly, make a plan for what you’ll do if your laptop or phone are lost or stolen or simply dies.   Is it insured?  Is it backed up?   I’ve seen too many people who never considered what would happen if they lost their device, and had to face a disastrous outcome when it happened.
